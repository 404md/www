What is Spherik Accelerator?

Spherik Accelerator is accepting application for the Summer 2017. It will take place in Cluj-Napoca, Romania from June – August 2017, with Demo day in September 2017. Main Selection Criteria: Passionate experienced entrepreneurs or ambitious first time founders with an early-stage prototype or MVP. Spherik Accelerator offers an intensive 4 months program designed to fine-tune tech products and provide teams with know-how and resources to go to market and expand their business globally.

Collaboration program

Validation: May 2017

Acceleration: June-August 2017

Demo day: September 2017

Intake: 10-15 teams

About program?

The program starts in May, with the validation phase, continues during June-August with the acceleration phase and ends in September with the Demo Day, when teams pitch in front of an audience that includes mentors, advisors, seasoned investors, representatives of the press and others. Designed for strong teams with awesome products targeting international markets, the programme provides hands-on assistance in product building, go-to market strategy, and stress-testing the business model.

During the program, teams will have access to:

  • 30+ mentors: domain specialists and top business leaders
  • 1-to-1 sessions with investors
  • office space & co-working
  • Free of charge services from partner companies (legal & financial advise, software & tools) Successful companies accelerated by Spherik agree to give us 3.14% out of their first investment received within 1 year after the program. These funds go back into the acceleration program to further develop and scale it.

What we look for

We welcome all technical start-ups that meet 3 conditions:

  • Teams have complementary business and tech skills or individuals have strong tech skills and a promising idea
  • Founders have the availability and are ready to take a commitment to fully engage in the acceleration process for the next 4 months
  • The product is an early-stage prototype or MVP with a clear R&D component in the technology area

We will particularly look at global focus areas like:

  • Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR)
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Robotics
  • Cyber Security
  • 3D Printing
  • Transportation / Mobility

AdTechMedia

AdTechMedia is on a mission to build the world’s most simple content monetization product. Imagine you are a publisher, a journalist or a blogger, and your core business is to create content that people love. Do you have extra time in your busy schedule to learn how to setup and manage Apple Pay or PayPal, Google Doubleclick For Publishers or Revive Ad Server, JavaScript or NodeJS? If the answer is strong NO, please continue reading...

AdTechMedia product simplifies content creators journey to revenue generation. In just couple of clicks, you can:

    (a) install and activate WordPress plugin from wordpress.org (or Drupal module from drupal.org), no technical skills required;
    (b) choose the revenue options your readers will be seamlessly provided;
    (c) customize any other look and feel settings that will make AdTechMedia plugin perform natively on your website.

Once readers land on your content webpage, our plugin will leave open just couple of paragraphs and lock the rest of the content, offering readers exactly two payment options out of three:

    (1) pay per article using micropayments
    (2) subscribe per month for unlimited consumption or
    (3) view an ad to continue reading for free.

ImpacT Academies & Camps

ImpacT Academies & Camps was created to give children the ability to get the digital skills needed to turn them from consumers to technological creators. Even if the child in the future will not activate in this sphere, these skills will help him achieve success in the contemporary world.

Using our own educational program, adapted from Silicon Valley schools, we teach not only technical skills - graphic design, programming, web sites, games, robotics, but also personal development skills: ability to manage time correctly, presentation skills, persuasion, negotiation, and others.

We are glad to extend the horizon of opportunities to the next generation, providing the skills needed to become a leader in the digital age. Join our trip!
